Causes of heart attack:

The most important point among the causes of heart attack is the accumulation of cholesterol plaques on the artery wall (atherosclerosis). As a result of this situation, hardening of the vessel walls will occur and the artery inner canal will narrow. If the narrowed veins cannot provide enough blood flow to the body, enough oxygenated blood cannot get to the heart muscle. Other answers to the question "What causes a heart attack?" Include unhealthy diet, excessive stress, genetic predisposition or smoking.

Many people continue to silently build up fat or plaque in the coronary arteries for years. Factors added on top of each other cause clogging of the vessels over time, especially smoking plays a role as a catalyst in this issue. Regardless of smoking men and women, the risk of heart attack is 3 times higher. Another reason for heart attack is the high rate of LDL, which is defined as bad cholesterol.Having diabetes can also increase the risk of heart attack. Other causes of heart attack can be listed as follows:

  • High blood pressure (hypertension)
  • Obesity
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Congenital heart diseases
  • Age (females> 55, males> 45)
